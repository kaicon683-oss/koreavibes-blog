## Welcome to Korea Vibe!

Planning a trip to Korea? You're in the right place.

Korea Vibe is your go-to guide for exploring South Korea like a local -- from curated travel itineraries to hidden cafes, must-try foods, and K-Drama filming locations you can actually visit.

### What You'll Find Here

**Itineraries** -- Step-by-step travel plans for Seoul, Busan, Jeju, and beyond. Each itinerary includes transportation tips, budget estimates, and recommended stops.

**Seoul Guides** -- Neighborhood-by-neighborhood guides to help you navigate Korea's capital city with confidence.

**Cafes & Food** -- The best Korean restaurants, street food spots, and aesthetic cafes worth visiting (and Instagramming).

**K-Drama Trips** -- Visit the real filming locations from your favorite Korean dramas. We include directions, photos, and scene references.

**Culture & Etiquette** -- Quick tips on Korean customs, useful phrases, and cultural do's and don'ts so you can travel respectfully.
