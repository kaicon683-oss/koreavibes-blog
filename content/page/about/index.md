## About Korea Vibe

Korea Vibe is an English-language travel blog dedicated to helping visitors explore South Korea with confidence and excitement.

### What We Cover

- **Travel Itineraries** -- Day-by-day plans for Seoul, Busan, Jeju, and more
- **Food & Cafe Guides** -- Where to eat, what to order, and how to find hidden gems
- **K-Drama Filming Locations** -- Visit the real places from your favorite shows
- **Cultural Tips** -- Navigate Korean customs, etiquette, and everyday life

### Why Korea Vibe?

Korea is an incredible travel destination, but planning a trip can feel overwhelming -- especially if you don't speak Korean. We break down everything into simple, actionable guides so you can spend less time planning and more time exploring.

Every guide includes:
- Exact subway directions and exit numbers
- Budget-friendly options alongside splurge picks
- Honest recommendations (no paid placements unless clearly disclosed)
